WishWallet believe that there are aspirations & wants that everyone of us have, which should be given more importance to, rather than traditional loan requirements against a pre stated purpose.

So, come on board this platform for a new age experience of needs fulfilment.
HOW IT WORKS ?

This application is for college students pursuing various degrees for fulfilment of their career goals. This generation has varied requirements and aspirations with ‘no explanation mindset’ but can manage their expenses well with the budget they have. We want to be part of their journey to help them in needs fulfilment till the time they enter country’s workforce.

Get On Board

Just login to WishWallet app, fill your profile and submit. All you need to sign up is your College Id and PAN/AADHAR Card.

Get Your Credit Limit

We don’t rely on your credit history if any, and instead rely on other data points via our proprietary algorithm. Once your profile approved get access to your credit limit.

Use Your Credit

Shop at more than 1,000 online websites or add money to your Paytm wallet! Transfer cash to bank account, head out on a trip, and lots more - pay for it later!

Repay & Repeat

Your card bill generates on the 1st of every month which you can choose to pay over some months by converting it into low cost EMIs or you just pay all of it at 0% interest.
